STORY

Voices. That is all Bob can hear. Voices inside his head and voices all around him. They call to him, reach out to him. For as long as he can remember, Bob has been haunted by these voices. Lately, these voices have been getting stronger. It seems so easy to give in to them. But Bob has not given up yet. He has one last chance to defeat these voices. One last chance to get his life back together. One last chance at...

REDEMPTION

How To Play

Use the arrow keys to move the character.

Use the spacebar key to use attacks.

Avoid the shadows which try to haunt Bob.

Objective

Your objective is to help guide Bob through the 5 stages of grief to one final stage...the stage of redemption.
